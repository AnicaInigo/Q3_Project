Fire Prevention Month is celebrated in the Philippines during the month of March wherein the weather is particularly hot and humid as it marks the start of summer. According to the data of the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP), March is also said to have the most fire incidents. By the mandate of Presidential Proclamation No. 115-A, s.1966, March is observed as the Fire Prevention Month to remind the public the importance of Fire Safety.


Here are some tips to prevent fires at home!



FIRE PREVENTION TIPS
Smoke Alarms

Install smoke alarms on every level of your home, inside bedrooms and outside sleeping areas. Make sure to test them every month and replace their batteries!
Avoid Smoking

Make sure cigarettes are disposed off properly and to never smoke in fire-prone areas.
NOTE: It's always better to stop smoking completely!
Cook Carefully

Never leave the cooking area unattended. Keep cloth, tissue, and other flammables away from the stove and keep them away from children's reach.
Electricity Safety

Make sure to unplug cords and replace those which are cracked or frayed. Don't overload extension cords.
Fire Escape Plan

Plan a Fire Escape Plan with your family and practice it at least twice a year!
